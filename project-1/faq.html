Frequently Asked Questions

My cube is malfunctioning. Can I get a refund?

Yes, you can. Our cubes have a 2-years warranty, so if the warranty hasn't expired, you can contact us to get your refund.

Do you have a physical store?

Currently, we only have an online store. We might open some physical stores in the future.

I live in Antarctica. Can I get a package delivered here?

I'm sorry, but we lack of way to send parcels to Antarctica. However, we're researching the possibility of sending packages to the Moon.

My parcel has delayed. Can I receive a discount?

Yes, you can. Depending on how many days the package was delayed. Please, contact us for more information.
